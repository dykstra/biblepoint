Parenting Train your children in the way of the Lord, and He will set their path straight. [Proverbs 22:6] Train up a child in the way he should go, even when he is old he will not depart from it. Your kids will imitate your ways; both individually and how you act toward one another.  Be a light to each other and them. You should be constantly praying for them, and with them; praying together as a family. It’s never too early to be praying for their salvation. We should never concern ourselves with what age a child can display true faith; read and share with them from the beginning. I encourage parents to shield their children at an early age from the bad things in this world.  Don’t buy into the talk about, oh they need to learn/be familiar with negative stuff to be strong, or they’ll see that kind of stuff sooner or later; that’s a terrible way to think and/or raise a child.  I’m a firm believer of what a person doesn’t know, he/she will not miss. Spend quality time with your kid(s), and focus on their biblical growth.
